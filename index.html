Explain How The Site Works 
    
      Explaining the steps to play a video without 
      annoying ads : 
      1 - Enter the YouTube application or click 
      on the YouTube icon above on the Right.
       It will take you to the YouTube main page.
      2 -Choose or search for the video you want to 
       watch.
      3 -Click on the share button and copy the video 
       link by clicking on the word (copy link ).
      4 -Go back to the site and paste the link you 
       copied into the designated place.
      5 -Click on the watch video button and 
      the video you want to watch will appear.
